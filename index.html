How much do you know about The Witcher 3?

First published during the '90s, Polish book series The Witcher follows the adventures of monster slayer Geralt of Rivia, his long-term lover and powerful sorceress Yennefer of Vengerberg and their adopted daughter Ciri, whose magic-infused birth granted her the ability to warp space and time. After the books garnered a cult following throughout Eastern Europe, Polish game development studio CD Projekt Red obtained the rights to convert pages to pixels in 2003. Following two successful prequels, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt received universal critical acclaim. Fans regard the third installment as a seminal work within the role-playing game genre for its compelling story, engrossing gameplay and detailed settings.
